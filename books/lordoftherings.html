The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien is an epic high fantasy trilogy that follows the journey of Frodo Baggins, a young hobbit tasked with destroying the One Ring, a powerful artifact forged by the dark lord Sauron.
Guided by his friends and allies, including Gandalf the wizard, Aragorn the ranger, and Samwise Gamgee, Frodo travels through Middle-earth, facing perilous landscapes, ancient evils, and internal struggles.
The story explores themes of friendship, bravery, and the corrupting nature of power. With richly detailed world-building and unforgettable characters, it remains one of the most celebrated and influential works in fantasy literature.
